Finger Training

Week 1: Establishing the Baseline

Saturday (Day 1): Moderate Climbing Session

Warm-up: 10–15 minutes of "arcing" (easy, continuous movement on large holds) and tendon glides.

Climbing Activity:

  • Intensity: One full grade below your on-sight level.
  • Terrain: Vertical to mildly overhanging.
  • Holds: Half crimps are permitted on large edges; avoid full crimping or high-velocity dynos.

Core: Floor Session (3 sets of 8–12 reps): Leg tucks, Copenhagen planks (each side), and back arches.

Sunday (Day 2): Mobility & Recovery

  • Tendon Glides: 5–10 minutes.
  • IASTM: Use a light tool (like a butter knife) for soft tissue mobilization.
  • Shoulder Stability: Cuban Rotations (3 sets of 12 reps) with light weights.
